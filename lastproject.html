Royal SMP Rules

To maintain a fair and enjoyable experience for everyone, please follow the rules below:

  1. No Combat Logging:
    Logging out during combat is not allowed. If you log out mid-fight, you must either:
    - Forfeit three items from your inventory, or
    - A trap will be placed at your last logout location.
  2. Base Protection:
    No griefing or stealing from other players' bases.
  3. Hacking & Duplication:
    Hacks or exploits are forbidden. Punishment includes:
    - 10-day ban,
    - Return of duped items,
    - Resource compensation to players.
